Pig Dice Game

How to Play:

  • Enter your names.
  • If it is your turn, click the 'roll' button to begin.
  • At any point, you may choose to 'stop' and collect your 'round tally' points.
  • If you roll a 1, your turn is over and you collect no points.
  • The first player to 50 total points wins!
